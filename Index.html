Why us


Our team is highly motivated and
adept in delivering problem-
solving, critical thinking, and soft
skills in technology that women
can apply in any STEM career path
as they seek employment.

Our Mission


To equip young Kenyan women in
marginalized communities with
employable coding skills, with the
aim of empowering them to have a
financially stable future and
contribute to the digital economy.

Women-Led


We create a holistic safe
environment where young women
have equitable opportunity in
technology training, mentorship,
and confidence to contribute in
the fast paced digital world.
